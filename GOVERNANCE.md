# Governance

Perturb-LM currently uses a two-person collaboration model.

## Roles

**Maintainer — Makenna Rodriguez (`@siamakenna`)**

- administers the repository and deployment;
- approves public scientific claims and releases;
- makes final decisions on benchmark definitions, licensing, and submissions;
- may use administrator bypass only for urgent repository recovery.

**Collaborator — `@siamakenna`**

- contributes through issues, branches, and pull requests;
- may lead agreed modeling, analysis, documentation, or engineering tasks;
- reviews maintainer-authored pull requests when appropriate;
- does not publish results or alter benchmark definitions without review.

## Decisions

Decisions affecting scientific interpretation, benchmark definitions, public claims, submission scope, authorship, or licensing must be recorded in an issue or pull request.

## Authorship and Credit

Authorship is not determined by GitHub access or commit count. Publication credit will be discussed separately based on intellectual and practical contributions.

## Conflict Resolution

The collaborators should document disagreements and evidence in an issue. Makenna retains final repository and public-release authority while the project remains under their account.
